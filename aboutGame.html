In "Accounting for Time," players step into the worn-out shoes of Daryl, an office worker trapped in a cycle of abuse under the tyranny of his boss, Mr. X. Daryl's days are filled with menial tasks, belittlement, and constant criticism, leaving him demoralized and exhausted. However, Daryl discovers an unexpected escape from his torment: his dreams.


"Accounting for Time" is not just a game but a poignant narrative experience that explores themes of oppression, self-discovery, and the power of the human spirit. Through its immersive gameplay and heartfelt storytelling, it invites players to embark on a journey of introspection and triumph, inspiring them to confront their own demons and emerge stronger on the other side.

"Accounting for Time" and how it's related to cycles...
"Accounting for Time" intricately weaves the theme of the cycle into its narrative and gameplay, reflecting the repetitive nature of Daryl's daily struggles and his journey towards breaking free from it.
  • Repetitive Dream Sequences: Wrapped in a monotonous routine of mistreatment at the hands of his boss, players must navigate through recurring dream sequences, each representing a different facet of Daryl's subconscious.

  • Breaking the Cycle: Throughout the game, players are challenged to break the cycle of abuse by helping Daryl confront his inner demons and ultimately confront his tyrant boss in his dreams. As players progress through the dreamworld, they unravel the layers of Daryl's subconscious, gaining insights into the root causes of his suffering and empowering him to break free from the oppressive cycle.

  • Reflection on Personal Cycles: Beyond Daryl's story, "Accounting for Time" prompts players to reflect on their own cycles and patterns in life. Through the immersive narrative and introspective gameplay, players are encouraged to examine their own struggles and consider how they can break free from their own cycles of adversity and self-doubt.

How to play...

🚶AWSD - movement
🎒I key - opens/closes inventory
👋Picking up items: left click
Inventory system: To use potions, open the inventory, hover over the potions (this will display a brief description of the item) and right-click
🗡️If you right-click on the sword, it will move to the quick slot panel (bottom center of the screen). After that, press 1 and it will appear in your hand. If you press 1 again, it will be hidden (but still in the quick slot panel)
🗡️Left-clicking with a sword in hand - hit
🏃Holding down the X key with AWSD - running
🤾Space - jump
⏸️The M key opens/closes the pause menu.
⚠️The pause menu is only available at the dungeon level
